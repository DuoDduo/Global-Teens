What Drives Us

Our mission and vision define the heartbeat of Global Teens

Our Mission

To educate, inspire, and equip teenagers with essential life skills through:

  • School outreach programs
  • Church partnerships
  • Awareness rallies
  • Purpose discovery workshops
  • Mentorship
Our Vision

To positively impact over 1,000 teenagers annually—guiding them to discover their identity, purpose, and passion.

We envision a generation of empowered teens transforming their communities through purpose-driven living.

About the Convener

The convener is passionate about shaping the future by investing in teenagers—helping them rise above limitations, discover purpose, and lead meaningful lives.

About Global Teens Summit

Global Teens Summit is an annual teenagers-centered initiative designed to educate, empower, and equip teenagers with relevant skills, mindset shifts, and mentorship to thrive in a purpose-driven life.

Our Objective

To create a supportive community where teenagers can discover their strengths, overcome challenges, and achieve their goals, becoming positive change-makers in their spheres of influence.
